The Company

About Argenta

A specialist Lloyd's market group with a history dating back to 1962, now wholly owned by Hannover Re and managing over £5.1 billion of investor capacity.

Who They Are

Argenta Holdings Ltd is a wholly owned subsidiary of Hannover Re, operating as an underwriting and insurance services group in the Lloyd's Market. The Group includes Argenta Syndicate Management Ltd (ASML) as a Lloyd's Managing Agent, and Argenta Private Capital Ltd (APCL), a specialist investment business managing over £5.1bn of investor capacity.

The Opportunity Within

This role sits in the Data and Reporting team, reporting to the Data and Reporting Manager. The data function is growing and investing in capability, giving you genuine scope to shape how the business derives value from its information assets. It is a strategic lead position with full product ownership from discovery through to delivery and ongoing support.

Culture and People

Argenta's employee engagement score is 75%, up from 56% in 2021. 96% of staff say their colleagues act with honesty and integrity, and 84% feel they can be themselves at work. Their long-standing workforce is described as their greatest asset, reflected in how they invest in benefits, development and wellbeing.

They provide insurance and reinsurance solutions across a broad range of classes, with global operations in London, Singapore, Sydney and Shanghai. The Opportunity

The Role

A strategic leadership position for a high performer ready to establish a legacy in a respected Lloyd's institution.

"We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Data Reporting Product Lead to join our growing data department. This role will act as the vital bridge between complex data infrastructure and actionable business insights. This is a strategic role for a high performer who is ready to establish a legacy."
Product Management
  • Act as the data reporting consultant, proactively identifying opportunities across all business domains and recommending pragmatic reporting solutions, both tactical and strategic.
  • Lead discovery by gathering requirements, refining them into technical specifications, and owning formal business sign-off.
  • Build POC mock-ups, MVPs and dashboards independently to validate ideas with stakeholders before productionising.
  • Serve as the primary interface between business and technical teams, articulating requirements to Data Engineers and Data Analysts.
  • Own the full delivery cycle including QA, UAT, deployment, end user training, and BAU support models.
Data Reporting Operations
  • Support the business through standardisation and automation of operational data reporting solutions.
  • Establish a Centre of Excellence covering Lloyd's regulatory reporting, audit reporting, and Finance Close reporting.
  • Collaborate with the Data Governance Lead on cross-functional data initiatives.
  • Define and track KPIs to measure the success of data reporting product delivery.
  • Support implementation of new technologies aligned to the organisation's longer-term ambitions.
Stakeholder Management
  • Navigate diverse priorities across the organisation, managing expectations and fostering collaboration between the central data hub and the wider business.
  • Champion data reporting practices and contribute to building a genuine data culture throughout the organisation.
  • Present at board level and work regularly with senior managers and subject matter experts.

What to Expect

The Interview Process

A clear three-stage process designed to be respectful of your time and give you every opportunity to show your best self.

1
Stage One

Introductory Call

A relaxed 20-minute conversation with your consultant at Eden James Consulting. Genuinely two-way, covering your Lloyd's experience, current motivations, and any questions you have about the role before committing further.

Prep tipNo formal prep required. Come ready to talk through your background and come with questions. Just be yourself.
2
Stage Two

Face to Face — Hiring Manager

An in-person meeting at the London office with the Data and Reporting Manager. Conversational in format, covering your CV, a walkthrough of the role, and a Q&A session. As much about you assessing them as the other way around.

Prep tipTell your career story clearly with strong examples of product ownership and stakeholder management in data roles. Prepare smart questions about the team's current priorities.
3
Stage Three

Technical and Competency Interview

A second face-to-face, likely with a team member present. Scenario-based and competency-based questions exploring how you think, how you solve problems, and how you have delivered in previous roles.

Prep tipUse the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Be ready for SQL, Power BI, data modelling, and Lloyd's regulatory reporting questions.
